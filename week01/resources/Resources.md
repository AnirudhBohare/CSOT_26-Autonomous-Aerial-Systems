## ✈️ Quadcopter Physics

| Resource | What it covers | Time |
|---|---|---|
| [How a Quadcopter Works (YouTube)](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=N_XneaFmOmU) | Roll/pitch/yaw, rotor directions — no math | 10 min |
| [Quadrotor Dynamics — Gibiansky](http://andrew.gibiansky.com/blog/physics/quadcopter-dynamics/) | Full 6-DOF mathematical derivation — closely matches what you'll implement | 20 min |
| [ETH Zurich Quadrotor Lecture Notes](https://www.flyingmachinearena.ethz.ch/wp-content/publications/2016/brescianini2016QuadcopterDyn.pdf) | Rigorous treatment of rotor mixing and dynamics | 30 min |
